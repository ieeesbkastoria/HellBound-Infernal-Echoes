##  Overview

*HellBound: Infernal Echoes* is a 2D action platformer inspired by *Danteâ€™s Inferno*, where players descend through the 9 Circles of Hell. With **procedurally generated levels and enemies**, every run offers a new challenge and experience.

## Gameplay Highlights

- **13 levels**, including a tutorial, with increasing difficulty.
- **3 unique bosses**, each guarding critical parts of Hell.
- **Procedural Generation**: Levels and enemies are different in every playthrough.
- **Level Up System**: Collect *Echoes* from defeated enemies to upgrade your character.
- **Player Abilities**:
  - Dash
  - Attack
  - Jump
  - Healing
  - Movement & Exploration
